    FAQ

    Frequently asked questions before moving to Spain.

    Can I use this if I only want to rent?

    Yes. The budget step changes when you choose rent, so you see monthly ranges instead of purchase amounts.

    Why do you ask for property type?

    An apartment, studio, penthouse and villa have very different budgets, areas and expectations. The first route should reflect that.

    Is this legal or tax advice?

    No. It is a first real estate orientation. Legal, tax and financing matters should be reviewed with the right professionals.

    What happens after I leave my details?

    A Mira Real Estate advisor can contact you to understand your timing, budget and preferred route in Spain.
